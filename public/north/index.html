For those of you who aren’t familiar with Jollof Rice (Jollof rice) —it is a rich

and incredibly rich, aromatic, tasty West African one-pot Meal.

It’s similar to Jambalaya but with distinctive African spices.

It consists mainly of cooked rice and tomato stew flavored with

spices such as thyme, scotch bonnet pepper, onions, and garlic.

The dish is a staple of West African cuisine, particularly that of Ghana,

Nigeria, Senegal, and Gambia often eaten and enjoyed during holidays,

weddings, birthdays, and other special events.
